Our story

E-waste is a crisis.
We built a solution.

E-Waste Recycling Manager started with a simple observation: people have old electronics, they know they shouldn't bin them, but there's no easy way to dispose of them properly.

So we built a platform that makes responsible recycling as simple as requesting a cab. No drop-off trips, no fees — schedule a pickup and we come to you.

57.4M
Metric tonnes of e-waste generated globally every year — and rising.
<20%
Of global e-waste is formally collected and recycled. The rest ends up in landfills.
The problem

E-waste is the fastest-growing waste stream on Earth

Electronic devices contain lead, mercury, cadmium, and other hazardous materials. When dumped in landfills, they contaminate soil and groundwater for decades.

At the same time, they contain gold, silver, copper, and rare earth metals — worth over $57 billion annually — that mostly go to waste.

70%
Of toxic landfill waste comes from discarded electronics, despite e-waste being only 2% of total waste volume.
1.8kg
CO₂ saved per laptop recycled responsibly vs. raw material extraction for a new device.
50+
Chemicals in a smartphone — many toxic, needing careful handling during disposal.
India
3rd largest e-waste producer globally, generating 3.2+ million metric tonnes annually.
Our mission

Make responsible e-waste disposal accessible to every household and business

The barrier to recycling should be zero. If it's easier to do the right thing than the wrong thing, people will choose right. That's the premise this platform is built on.

The platform

Three portals. One system.

Designed for three distinct users — clients with e-waste, agents who collect it, and admins who keep everything running smoothly.

Client Portal
For individuals & businesses with e-waste
  • Register for free with your email
  • Submit pickup requests with photos
  • Review and accept agent price offers
  • Track your pickup in real time
  • Download your recycling certificate
Agent Portal
For certified collection agents
  • Apply and get verified by admin
  • Receive admin invitations for nearby pickups
  • Submit competitive price offers
  • Manage your pickup schedule
  • Mark collections as completed
Admin Portal
For E-Waste Recycling Manager administrators
  • Manage all clients and agents
  • Approve or revoke agent accounts
  • Invite agents by locality to make offers
  • View all pickup requests and statuses
  • Manage e-waste categories, cities & states
  • Respond to support messages
