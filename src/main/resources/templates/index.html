Process

Three steps to a cleaner planet

No drop-off hunting. No confusing steps. Just register, submit, and we handle the rest.

Start recycling →
1
Register and describe your items
Create a free account and tell us what you want to dispose of — laptops, phones, TVs, batteries. Upload a photo and set a pickup window.
2
Agent offers a price and visits
Admin invites nearby certified agents to make price offers. You review the offers and accept the best deal. The agent collects from your door.
3
Collected — get your certificate
Once collected, your items are responsibly processed. Download your digital recycling certificate as proof of responsible disposal.

Impact

Every device matters

E-waste is the world's fastest-growing waste stream. A single laptop contains lead, mercury, and cadmium. Proper recycling keeps toxins out of soil and water — and recovers valuable metals.
